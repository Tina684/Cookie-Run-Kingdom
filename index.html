Facts About Cookie Run: Kingdom

Cookie Run: Kingdom is a game made by Devsisters. This game is one of the 5 games they made, Cookie Run: OvenBreak, Cookie Run: Puzzle World, Solitaire: Decked out, and Tape it Up! are the other games they made. Cookie Run: Kingdom is based off of different types of sweets and fruits.

Run: Kingdom has cookies as characters, soulstones that lets them reach to 5 stars, skill powder to make their skills more powerful, star jellies to level them up, rainbow cubes that can purchase pretty and cool costumes that fits the new events, treasures that help boost your cookies during battles, toppings that gives special effects to cookies, and cookie cutters that allows you to get cookies and soul stones.

Cookie Run: Kingdom has lots of battles, including world exploration, cookie alliance, bounties, kingdom arenas, guild battles, tower of sweet choas, and tropical soda islands.
