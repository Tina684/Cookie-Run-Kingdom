Events and Other Things

  • Run: Kingdom will always have small events daily to keep you busy.
  • Big events come every update and the home screen will update too.
  • Tree of Wishes allows you to get coins and kingdom level by making cookie's wishes come true.
  • Sugar Gnome Laboratory allows you to research about your kingdom and cookies giving them boosts like decreasing time or incresing health or defense.
  • Touc's Trade Harbor allows you to trade your goods to get rainbow pearls which allows you to get legendary cookie's soul stones and any other things they sell, this shop updates every 2-3 days.
  • Fountain of Abundance allows you to get free goods including skill power, coins, stamina jellies, and star jellies.

    • Battles

    In order to battle in world explorations you need stamina jellies, to battle in cookie alliance you need alliance tickets, you can only battle in bounties 3 times a day to get the skill powder those days provide. To battle in kingdom arenas you need arena tickets, you can only battle in guild battles 3 times a day, to battle in tower of sweet choas you need searing keys, and you can battle in tropical soda islands as long as you opened enough space but it will make you battle again after you finish making that island yours. Your tickets, keys and stamina jellies will always regenerate.
