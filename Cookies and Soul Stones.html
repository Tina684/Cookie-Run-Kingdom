Cookies

  • are the characters in this game.
  • Cookies can be obtained through cookie rolls. 300 diamonds for one roll, 3,000 for 10 rolls. You can also get them from Special Cookie Cutter and normal Cookie Cutters. Special Cutters increases the chance to get the event cookie or their soulstones.
  • Every main update has one to two new cookies.
  • Cookies and their soulstones have rarities, they either are Ancient, Legendary, Epic, Rare, or Common.
  • There are 2 ancients cookies that can be obtained, Pure Vanilla Cookie and Hollyberry Cookie.
  • With the new update there are 2 legendary cookies, Sea Fairy Cookie and Frost Queen Cookie.

    • Soul Stones

    • Soul Stones are another way to get cookies.
    • With 20 Soul Stones you can unlock a cookie.
    • Soul Stones are also for stars, 1 star needs 20 stones, 2 star needs 30, 3 star needs 50, 4 star needs 70, and 5 star needs 100.
