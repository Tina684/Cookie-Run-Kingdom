Toppings

  • There are different types of toppings.
  • Toppings are used like boosts, for example, chocolate is used for shortening cool down speed, raspberry is for increase attack, and walnut is for defense.
  • There are Epic, Rare, and Common toppings but Epic toppings have a higher percentage of the skill it is used for.

    • Skill Powders

    Skill Powders are used to level up your cookie's skill. It can be obtained from the Fountain of Abundance and bounties or you can buy it using rainbow pearls.

    Star Jellies

    Star Jellies are used for leveling up your cookies, there are 8 levels of star jellies. When you first enter the game, they will provide you with some jellies. The max level for any cookie is level 60. You can get level one star jellies from Cookie Houses and other level jellies from purchasing or from daily award.
