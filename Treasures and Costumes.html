Treasure

  • Treasures are helpful for battle.
  • Treasures have rarities, they either are Epic, Rare, or Common.
  • There are 40 Epic cookies, 14 Rare, and 7 Common Treasures at this point.

    • Costumes

    Costumes don't have any effects but they are very pretty and cool. Updates sometimes come with costumes. Costumes can purchased by using rainbow cubes but the Epic costumes are much harder to get. You need 300 rainbow cubes to draw one time and 1500 cubes to draw 5 times. There are Common, Rare, and Epic costumes. Sadly, not all cookie have a costume.
